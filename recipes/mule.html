Limoncello Moscow Mule

Description:

An easier version of the Moscow Mule drink, but still prettty tasty

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup ice
  • ½ fluid ounce fresh lime juice
  • 1 ½ fluid ounces limoncello liqueur
  • 1 ½ fluid ounces vodka
  • 5 ounces ginger beer
  • 1 thin lemon slice (optional)

Preparation:

  1. Fill a copper mule mug with ice. Add lime juice, limoncello, and vodka; stir to combine. Top with ginger beer. Lightly stir again and garnish with lemon slice.
